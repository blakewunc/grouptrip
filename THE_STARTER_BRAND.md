# The Starter — Brand & Copy Guidelines

## What it is

The Starter is GroupTrip's golf module. It handles tee time coordination,
handicap-adjusted betting formats (Nassau, skins, wolf), live scoring,
payout calculations, and equipment management.

## Name context

- Always "The Starter" with a capital T — not "the starter" or "Starter"
- Domain: thestarter.app
- Tagline: "Golf trips, handled."

## Voice & tone

- Confident, dry, slightly irreverent — like a caddie who's seen it all
- Short sentences. Active voice.
- Golf-literate but not exclusionary — gender neutral, no laddish energy
- Never startup-y or over-enthusiastic
- The Starter runs the show — that's the energy

## Key copy lines (use these, don't rewrite them)

| Use case      | Copy                                              |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Tagline       | "Golf trips, handled."                            |
| Value prop    | "You book the tee times. We handle everything else." |
| Invite hook   | "[Name] just sent you off."                       |
| Payout        | "No more Venmo math. The Starter does it."        |

## Words to avoid

- "guys", "boys", "brotherhood" — keep it group-neutral
- "seamless", "powerful", "robust" — generic tech filler
- Anything that sounds like a Salesforce feature announcement

## Visual direction

- Warm off-white `#F5F1ED`, blue-gray accent `#70798C`
- No gradients
- Abercrombie & Kent luxury feel — understated, confident
- Golf-adjacent imagery: course, scorecard, green — never stock photo golfers

## Relationship to GroupTrip

- The Starter is a module within GroupTrip, not a standalone product
- Always position as "The Starter by GroupTrip" in external contexts
- The viral entry point is the invite/proposal page — every design
  decision should make that page more compelling to a first-time visitor
